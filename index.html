Welcome to Spray Water Park, Jaipur's ultimate destination for splashy fun and unforgettable memories!

Located in the heart of Mouja Ri Dhani Jagatpura, Spray Water Park offers an exhilarating experience for families, friends, and corporates alike. Our state-of-the-art water park features thrilling slides, wave pools, and relaxing attractions, ensuring something for every age and thrill level.

Join us for a day of adventure, excitement, and rejuvenation. Book now and make a splash at Spray Water Park!
